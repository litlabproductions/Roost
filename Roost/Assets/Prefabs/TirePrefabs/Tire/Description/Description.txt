Low poly tire with pbr material.
Material contain 3 textures(2048*2048px): diffuse (Albedo), normal map, metallic texture (with adjusted smoothness)
All textures are applied and adjusted.
Mesh is fitted to Unity measurement system.

	***How to use***
Just drag and drop model from "Prefabs" folder into the scene view or hierarchy window. If necessary, add needed components, like rigidbody or collider.
